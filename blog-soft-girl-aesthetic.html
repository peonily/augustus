Seaonal Trends: The Riviera Look

By AUGUSTUS Editorial | March 4, 2026

Start With The Jacket

A Riviera wardrobe begins with a jacket that feels easy but still frames the body. Choose breathable linen, cotton, or a soft wool blend in navy, sand, or light grey, then let the fabric carry the elegance.

Build Your Palette

  • Base tones: white, ecru, stone, and navy
  • Accent tones: tobacco, olive, pale blue, and washed denim
  • Grounding tones: black leather, dark brown suede, and charcoal

Prioritize Fabric And Fit

The look works when the clothes move naturally. Shirts should breathe, trousers should skim rather than cling, and knitwear should be fine enough to layer under a jacket without bulk.

Five Piece Formula For Easy Styling

A practical starter set includes one linen shirt, one knit polo, one unstructured blazer, one tailored trouser, and one pair of loafers. Rotate belts, sunglasses, and watches to shift the tone from daytime casual to evening refined.

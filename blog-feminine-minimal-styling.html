Seaonal Trends: The Riviera Look

By AUGUSTUS Editorial | March 4, 2026

Light Tailoring Leads The Look

The Riviera look is built around ease without losing precision. Start with a linen blazer, an open-collar shirt, or a fine knit polo, then keep the rest of the outfit clean so the silhouette feels relaxed but intentional.

Three Styling Anchors

  • Proportion: soft shoulder, neat waist, and trousers with movement
  • Color: navy, white, stone, tobacco, and pale blue
  • Texture: linen, cotton, suede, woven leather, and fine knitwear

Accessory Editing

Keep accessories purposeful: loafers, a leather belt, a slim watch, or dark sunglasses. The outfit should look ready for travel, dinner, and a waterfront walk without changing the base.

Daily Formula For Consistency

Pair a crisp shirt with tailored trousers, add a light jacket when needed, and finish with loafers or minimal sneakers. Repeating that formula keeps summer dressing sharp without feeling overworked.
